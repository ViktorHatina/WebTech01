Beef Nachos

This recipe will blow your mind!

Occassion

Having people over and looking for the ideal snack to fill the table? Nachos will hit the spot

Ingredients

Ground Beef 500g
Garlic (2cloves)
Onion
Peppers
Taco Seasoning
Nachos (3bags)
Nacho salsa
Cheddar Cheese (200g)
Mozzarrella Cheese(150g)

Instructions

Chop up your onions and peppers and fry them till brown on a buttered pan. Once brownish, Add two tea spoons of taco seasoning. Mix, and start slowly adding ground beef. Stir for 10minutes on high heat. Cover a oven tray in tinfoil and add the nacho chips. Add cheese and fried beef mix over the top. Mix with salsa, and cover the top with remaining cheese. Bake in oven over medium heat for 5minutes.
Enjoy!!
