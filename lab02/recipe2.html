Vegan Mac & Cheese

This recipe will blow your mind!

Occassion

Want to take a break from meat and dairy products? We have the best vegan dish for any occassion.

Ingredients

Carrots (10)
Garlic (2cloves)
Shallots
Pasta (500g)
Miso paste (3spoons)
Keshu nuts (200g)
Bread crumbs (optional topping)

Instructions

Chop up your shallots and simmer them in a pot with butter. Peel and chop up your carrots. Once your shallots get a brownish tint, add the carrots and 2cups of water. Grind up the Keshus and add them to the pot. Boil for 5minutes. Once soft, mix and add miso paste. Add a cup of water and continue cooking. Cook the pasta and mix everything together.
Enjoy!!
